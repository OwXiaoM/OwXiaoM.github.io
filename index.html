Hello, my name is Xiaoming, it's a pleasure to meet you.
Photography is a passion of mine, which naturally extends to my enthusiasm for low-level vision tasks. Engaging in these tasks has deepened my understanding of digital imaging and the art of photography.
I am currently collaborating with Professor Le Lu in medical image analysis, with a focus on addressing the challenges with liver diseases.
In my journey through the field of computer vision, I am immensely grateful for the opportunity to have been guided by Professor Tianrui Li, and Pascal Prea.
Below, I made a small collection of my scenic photographs. Capturing these images has been a soothing and enjoyable experience, and I hope they bring you joy as well.
