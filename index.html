Hello, my name is Xiaoming, it's a pleasure to meet you. Photography is a passion of mine, which naturally extends to my enthusiasm for low-level vision tasks in the realm of computer vision. Engaging in these tasks has deepened my understanding of digital imaging and the art of photography. In my journey through the field of computer vision, I am immensely grateful for the opportunity to have been guided by Tianrui Li, Xiaole Zhao, Shuaicheng Liu, and Pascal Prea. Presently, I'm embarked on a voyage of discovery, and hope to advance a little for the computer vision community. Below, I made a small collection of my scenic photographs. Capturing these images has been a soothing and enjoyable experience, and I hope they bring you joy as well.
