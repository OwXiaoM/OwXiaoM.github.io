Hello, my name is Xiaoming, it's a pleasure to meet you.
My research interest lies in medical image analysis, with a focus on addressing diagnostic challenges and ageing.
I am now contributing to the ERC Consolidator Grant Project CARAVEL, together with Professor Maria A. Zuluaga.
I collaborated with Professor Le Lu at Alibaba DAMO Academy, where I worked with Dr. Yu Shi.
I am always grateful for the foundational guidance I received from Professor Tianrui Li and Professor Pascal Prea during my journey in computer vision.
